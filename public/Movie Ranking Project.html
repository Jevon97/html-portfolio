The Best Anime According to Jevon

My Top 3 Anime of all time.

Sword Art Online

Sword Art Online or SAO is a sci-fi, fantasy, action anime. This anime is the best anime ever because of the quality and the originality of the story.

Hunter X Hunter

Hunter X Hunter is another best fantasy and action anime. The story revolves around friends and family, it's a great anime to teach you to never give up.

Spirited Away

Even though Spirited Away made in the year 2001, the quality of the anime movies made by Studio Ghibli is not to mess about. The originality of the story, the message from it, and the details is just stunning.
